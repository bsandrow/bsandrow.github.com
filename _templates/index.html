Biography

I was born in Dearborn, Michigan in April 1982. I grew up in the area, and eventually graduated from the University of Michigan with a B.S. in Computer Science. After graduation, I moved to Toronto, Ontario to be with my girlfriend (now wife), Angela.

I took a job for Rentrak Corp. in August 2008 based on the recommendation of a friend from Michigan who was working there. The job was meant to be temporary, but my wife ended up moving out with me and we were there for 3 years. While we were there, our daughter Amelie was born. I even attempted to break into programming prior to high-school, but it wasn't until AP Computer Science class (in grade 11) that I started in earnest.
